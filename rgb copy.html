The RGB System

The Red‑Green‑Blue colour model encodes every hue your screen can display as three integers: R, G, B ∈ 0‑255. That’s 2563 = 16 777 216 unique combinations.

We’re turning that spectrum into a 256 × 256 × 256 voxel multiverse. Owning a colour means owning a coordinate—your voxel in the RGB space. Collaborators and brands will interact with this space for art drops, virtual fashion and data visualisations.
