The RGB System

2563 = 16 777 216 colours

The Red‑Green‑Blue model represents every screen hue as three integers (0‑255). Multiply those ranges (256 × 256 × 256) and you get 16 777 216 unique combinations.

A 256 × 256 × 256 voxel universe

We’re turning that spectrum into a cubic world. Owning a colour means owning one voxel—your coordinate in RGB space. Brands and creators will build art drops, virtual fashion and data visualisations anchored to these voxels.
